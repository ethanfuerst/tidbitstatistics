Now that I have established my ratings, let's talk about IMDb's rating system. Currently, the highest rated movie on IMDb is [The Shawshank Redemption](https://www.imdb.com/chart/top/?ref_=nv_mv_250), with a 9.2/10. I wish that I could rate movies with a decimal rating and not just a whole number partially because the average ratings are rounded to one decimal place.

When I compare an IMDb rating with my rating though, I get to see which movies I liked more than IMDb and less than IMDb.

#### So what actually is my taste in movies?

Whenever someone asks me what my taste in movies is, I usually say that my favorite kind of movie is a 2010's Sci-Fi movie. I also tell them I'm not a huge fan of older movies. But is this actually the case? Do I really like new movies more and do I really like sci-fi movies the most?

First, I'm gonna see when most of the movies I have rated were released.
